convert_us2uk R Documentation

Convert words from US to UK English

Description

convert_us2uk converts a vector of words from US to UK English.

Usage

convert_us2uk(x, crosswalk = uk2us::ukus_crosswalk)

Arguments

x

Vector of words to convert.
crosswalk

Data frame with columns uk and us.

Value

A vector of converted words.

See Also

convert_uk2us

Examples

convert_us2uk(c("center", "color", "colorize"))
