fin_ticker R Documentation

Open a ticker in yahoo finance.

Description

Open a stock ticker, currently in yahoo finance

Usage

fin_ticker(ticker = "INTC")

Arguments

ticker

A stock symbol to look up, e.g., "OXY"

Value

  • Open a ticker in a finance site online
