armaFilter

Description

Filter of time series

Usage

armaFilter(MA = 1, AR = 1, y)

Arguments

MA

numerator polynomial
AR

denominator polynomial
y

a vector, ts or tsibble object

Value

Filtered time series

Author(s)

Diego J. Pedregal

y <- armaFilter(1, c(1 , -0.8), rnorm(200))
